Jim McEvoy

All-Around Problem Solver

Born in Wisconsin, Jim went through several moves with his family before landing in the Howell area. A graduate of MSU and a lifelong Spartan, Jim began his career in social work before coming to Optum in 2006. From humble beginnings as an ETL and .NET developer, he quickly rose to a senior level as a data analyst, database developer and web designer/developer. Jim has played a major role as an analyst and tech lead in many high-visibility DHHS/Optum projects during his tenure.

When not coding, Jim’s interests include all things Spartan- related, particularly football and basketball; he is one-half of the team producing the “Spartan Upper Deck” podcast during football season. He’s an avid home brewer and has produced a number of tasty beer varieties, at least as far as he can remember. He enjoys traveling and camping with his wife Carin and three children Clara, Sadie and Miles.

While we will miss him, we wish him well and thank him for his many contributions. Be aware, however, that we may not be entirely rid of him; many of us will cross paths with him in the future in his new role at Health Management Associates.
